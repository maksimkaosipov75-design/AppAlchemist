# Code of Conduct

AppAlchemist follows a simple rule: be respectful, practical, and evidence-driven.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming to new Linux users and maintainers.
- Keep issue reports focused on reproducible facts.
- Assume good intent when discussing package failures or platform differences.
- Share logs, package versions, and environment details when they matter.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing bug reports without evidence
- Posting malicious packages or unsafe instructions
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that make the project unsafe or unproductive.
